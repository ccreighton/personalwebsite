About Me

Hi, I'm Chad. I love lifting, building bikes for kids that don't have them, and doing some fun work on developing projects for niche areas. I love problem-solving and moving quickly to failure for feedback loops. In the past, I've worked at a grocery store, a professional golf caddy, a COO in a number of startups, and now I work independently as support for cybersecurity and forensic work as well as recently getting into the trenches of AI frameworks and developing processes around it. I enjoy pushing my own boundaries into areas I know little of. I also write, often, because if I don't I'll go crazy.
